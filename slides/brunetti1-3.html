Why a Cartooning Studio?

In the first half of the course, we will use Ivan Brunetti's Cartooning: Philosophy and Practice and Scott McCloud's Understanding Comics as guides to prototyping our own graphic work. Simultaneously, we will read Nick Drnaso's Sabrina. We will use our own comics making to generate questions we might explore in the process of writing about Drnaso's novel.
